# Dokumentasi Lapangan - Quick Start (2 Minutes)

**Duration**: 2 minutes ⏱️  
**Difficulty**: Beginner ⭐  
**Goal**: Upload field activity documentation images to your form

---

## What is Dokumentasi Lapangan?

Simple: Upload up to **2 photos** documenting your field inspection activities, add descriptions, and they appear in your PDF report.

**Example uses**:
- Document the overall inspection area
- Show weather conditions
- Capture team at work
- Document special observations

---

## Step-by-Step (2 minutes)

### 1️⃣ Go to Your Form (30 seconds)

```
Dashboard 
  → Forms 
    → Create New Form (or Edit Existing)
      → Scroll down...
```

### 2️⃣ Find "📸 Dokumentasi Lapangan" Section (30 seconds)

You'll see a blue upload box with text:
```
📷
Klik untuk upload dokumentasi lapangan
```

### 3️⃣ Upload Your First Image (30 seconds)

- **Click** the upload area
- **Select** an image file (JPG, PNG)
- **Wait** for upload to complete

### 4️⃣ Add Caption (30 seconds)

```
Image appears in grid below upload area:
┌─────────────────┐
│   [Your Image]  │
├─────────────────┤
│ Keterangan:     │
│ [Type here...] │  ← Write what you see in image
└─────────────────┘
```

Example captions:
- "Dokumentasi aktivitas inspeksi area runway"
- "Kondisi cuaca saat inspeksi - cerah"
- "Tim inspeksi di lapangan"

### 5️⃣ Optional: Upload Second Image (30 seconds)

- Click upload area again
- Select another image
- Add caption
- **Note**: Can upload max 2 images only

### 6️⃣ Save Form (Auto)

Click **"Simpan"** button at bottom to save everything.

---

## That's It! ✅

Your documentation images are now:
- ✅ Saved in database
- ✅ Will appear in PDF report (in their own page)
- ✅ Can be edited/deleted anytime

---

## View in PDF

When you download the PDF report:

```
Page 1: Checklist
Page 2-N: Inspection Form (8 sections)
Page N+1: 📸 DOKUMENTASI LAPANGAN ← Your images appear here!
         - Image 1 + Your caption
         - Image 2 + Your caption (if uploaded)
Page N+2: Inspection Photos (FOD - if any)
Final: Signatures
```

## Common Questions

### Q: How many images can I upload?
**A**: Maximum **2 images** per form.

### Q: Can I delete an image after uploading?
**A**: Yes! Click the red "🗑 Hapus" button under the image.

### Q: Can I edit the caption?
**A**: Yes! Click in the caption field and edit anytime before saving.

### Q: What image formats are supported?
**A**: JPG, PNG, and any common image format.

### Q: Can I reorder the images?
**A**: Currently no, but you can delete and re-upload in desired order.

### Q: Will the images appear in the PDF?
**A**: Yes! On a dedicated "DOKUMENTASI LAPANGAN" page before FOD images.

### Q: Can I delete images after saving the form?
**A**: Yes! Edit the form anytime and delete using "🗑 Hapus" button.

## Troubleshooting

### "Upload button disabled" or "Maksimal 2 gambar"
- You've already uploaded 2 images
- Delete one first, then upload new one

### "Gagal mengupload gambar" (Upload failed)
- Check internet connection
- Try different image file
- Refresh page and try again

### Image not showing in PDF
- Check if form was saved (get success message)
- Try downloading PDF again
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

### Caption not saving
- Click outside caption field to auto-save
- Click "Simpan" to save entire form

## Tips & Best Practices

✅ **DO**
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Write descriptive captions
- Document overall scene, not just details
- Upload before approval for completeness

❌ **DON'T**
- Upload blurry or dark images
- Use 3+ images (max is 2)
- Leave captions empty (use captions for context)
- Forget to save form when done
